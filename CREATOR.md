# Creator

Social Demand Signal Agent was created by [Jack Dalrymple](https://www.jackdalrymple.com/), Founder of [Cap & Cut](https://capandcut.com/).

Jack is a creative-growth and marketing operator with more than 20 years of experience building campaigns, positioning systems, community programs, content pipelines, and go-to-market engines across gaming, entertainment, emerging technology, AI, and Web3.

His work connects:

- customer insight and market positioning
- creative systems and campaign execution
- community-first growth
- agentic workflows and AI-enabled operations
- experimentation and learning systems
- narrative, adoption, and go-to-market strategy

Social Demand Signal Agent reflects a practical operating belief: social listening becomes more valuable when a team can move from customer words to governed action, then learn from customer behavior instead of vanity metrics.
